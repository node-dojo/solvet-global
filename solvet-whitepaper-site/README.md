## Documentation

### White Paper (`SOLVET_WHITEPAPER.md`)
Complete technical and business documentation covering:
- Problem statement and market opportunity
- Solution overview and technical architecture
- Business model and competitive analysis
- Implementation roadmap and technology stack

### Development Roadmap (`SOLVET_ROADMAP.md`)
Detailed technical roadmap with Mermaid diagrams:
- Phase-by-phase implementation timeline
- Component development flow
- Integration dependencies
- Risk management and success metrics

### Investor Roadmap (`SOLVET_INVESTOR_ROADMAP.md`)
Concise investor-facing document highlighting:
- Investment thesis and market opportunity
- Business model and unit economics
- Growth trajectory and financial projections
- Investment ask and ROI projections

### Website (`website/`)
One-pager SaaS pitch website:
- Hero section with value proposition
- Problem/Solution comparison
- Key features and market opportunity
- Business model and growth trajectory
- Investment opportunity and CTA

## Design System

The website follows the NO3D Tools design system:
- **Fonts:** Visitor TT1 BRK for headers, JetBrains Mono for body
- **Colors:** Lello (#f0ff00) for accents, Black (#000000) for primary text
- **Spacing:** 10px base unit system
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, minimal, terminal-inspired design
