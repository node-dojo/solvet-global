# NO3D Tools Subscription - User Guide

## Getting Started

### Subscribing

1. Visit the [Subscribe page](https://no3dtools.com/subscribe.html)
2. Review the benefits and pricing
3. Click "Subscribe Now"
4. Complete checkout through Polar
5. You'll be redirected to a success page with next steps

### Accessing Your Account

1. Visit the [Account page](https://no3dtools.com/account.html)
2. Sign in with your Polar account if prompted
3. View your subscription status and download the library

## Downloading the Library

### First Download

1. Go to your Account page
2. Click "Download Library" button
3. Wait for the ZIP file to be prepared (may take a moment)
4. The download will start automatically
5. Extract the ZIP file to access all products

### Updating Your Library

When new products are added to the library:

1. You'll see an "Update Available" badge on your Account page
2. Click "Download Library" again to get the updated version
3. The new download includes all products (existing + new)
4. Extract and use the updated library

## Managing Your Subscription

### View Subscription Status

- Visit your Account page to see:
  - Current subscription status (Active/Expired)
  - Renewal date
  - Plan details

### Update Payment Method

- Click "Manage Billing" on your Account page
- This redirects to Polar's customer portal
- Update your payment method or billing information

### Cancel Subscription

- Click "Manage Billing" on your Account page
- Go to Polar's customer portal
- Cancel your subscription
- Access continues until the end of your billing period

## Using the Products

### After Download

1. Extract the downloaded ZIP file
2. Browse the product folders (organized by category)
3. Each product folder contains:
   - `.blend` file (Blender asset)
   - `.json` file (metadata)
   - Icon images
   - Optional: description markdown, video preview

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Download Library

- **Check subscription status**: Ensure your subscription is active
- **Clear browser cache**: Try refreshing the page
- **Check internet connection**: Large downloads require stable connection
- **Contact support**: If issues persist

### Update Badge Not Clearing

- The badge clears after you download the updated library
- If it persists, try refreshing the Account page
- Ensure you've completed the download

### Subscription Shows Expired

- Check your payment method in Polar customer portal
- Ensure payment was successful
- Contact support if payment was made but status is incorrect

## FAQ

**Q: Do I get access to new products automatically?**  
A: Yes! Your subscription includes all current and future products added while your subscription is active.

**Q: Can I download multiple times?**  
A: Yes, you can download the library as many times as you need.

**Q: What happens if I cancel?**  
A: You keep access until the end of your billing period. After that, you'll need to resubscribe to access downloads.

**Q: Can I use products commercially?**  
A: Yes, all products come with a commercial license included with your subscription.

**Q: How often are new products added?**  
A: New products are added regularly. You'll be notified when updates are available.
